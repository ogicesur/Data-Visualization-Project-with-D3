Welcome to our InfoVis Project

With this website we intend to visualize how COVID-19 affected the tourism within all federal states of Germany.
You can view information from 2019, the year before COVID-19, and 2020, where the pandemic started and
how the established COVID restrictions (e.g. lockdown, etc) influenced the tourism. The main focus lies
on the number of people that arrived (Arrivals) and people the stayed overnight (Overnight Stays) during the time period.
For reference the COVID restrictions during 2020 were
1. Lockdown: 16.03.2020 - 19.04.2020
2. Lockdown: 02.11.2020 - 20.12.2020
